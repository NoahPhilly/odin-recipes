Baked Chicken Recipe!

Delicious baked chicken recipe the family will love!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of raw chicken
  • Desired salt and pepper

Steps!

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F
  2. Grease pan to prevent sticking
  3. Bake chicken for 15-20 minutes
  4. Let chicken rest for a couple of minutes
  5. Enjoy!
